MODIFIED FROM OKUDA ET AL. 2002 (mostly osteology):

1. Body volume (BV)

2. Standard length (SL):
	- Anterior tip of lower jaw to caudal peduncle

3. Head length (HL)
	- Anterior tip of lower jaw to posterior of operculum

4. Snout length (SNL): 
	- Anterior tip of upper jaw to  anterior edge of orbit

5. Lower jaw length (LJL):
	- Anterior tip of dentary to posterior of articular

6. Dentary-hypohyal length (Dnt-Hyp):
	- Anterior tip of dentary to posterior of hypohyal

7. Head depth (HD):
	- Posterior tip of premaxillary pedical to anterior tip of hypohyal

8. Mouth width (MW):
	- Posterior tips of left and right maxillae

9. Angular width (AW): 
	- Lateral edges of left and right angulars

10. Urohyal length (UHL):
	- Anterior to posterior tips of urohyal

11. Urohyal height (UHH):
	- Deepest point of urohyal

12. Sternohyoideus (STNH):
	- Dorsal and ventral points of origin; attachment site
